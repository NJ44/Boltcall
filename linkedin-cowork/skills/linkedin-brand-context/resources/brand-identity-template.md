## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Who reads your content and might buy from you. Be specific — the more precise, the better the hooks.

```
ICP Title/Role: [e.g. "B2B SaaS founders", "freelance designers", "marketing leaders at Series A startups"]
ICP Company Stage: [e.g. "bootstrapped", "seed to Series B", "enterprise"]
ICP Problem They Have: [e.g. "They're posting on LinkedIn but getting no leads"]
ICP Desired Outcome: [e.g. "Build a personal brand that generates inbound pipeline"]
ICP Fears/Frustrations: [e.g. "Wasting time on content that gets no traction", "Looking unprofessional"]
```

## Voice & Tone

How you write. Claude will match this in every post, DM, and carousel.

```
Voice style: [e.g. "Direct, no fluff, data-driven" / "Warm, mentor-like, storytelling" / "Edgy, contrarian, challenge-everything"]
Sentence length: [e.g. "Short punchy sentences. Max 12 words."]
Words you use: [e.g. "systems, leverage, asymmetric, operator"]
Words you NEVER use: [e.g. "synergy, impactful, utilize, holistic, journey"]
Phrases that sound like you: [e.g. "The math is simple." / "Here's what nobody tells you:"]
Phrases that DON'T sound like you: [e.g. "In conclusion", "It's important to note", "As we navigate"]
Use emojis? [Yes / No / Sparingly]
```
