## How to Read This Library

Each entry is one analyzed lead magnet. Claude adds a new entry after every `/linkedin-analyze-lead-magnet` run.

The library is searchable by: creator, content type, score, niche, and date.

## Notes on Library Use

**For competitive analysis:** Sort by niche + score. High-scoring magnets from competitors = study their best ideas, not their format.

**For content ideas:** The "Biggest Gap" column across 10+ entries shows exactly what your niche is missing — that's your next lead magnet.

**For ICP research:** Look at what lead magnets in your niche promise vs. deliver. The delta = your audience's biggest unsolved problem.
