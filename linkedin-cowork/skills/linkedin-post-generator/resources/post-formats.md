# Post Formats — 6 LinkedIn Format Templates

> LinkedIn rewards native content that keeps people on the platform. These 6 formats are the highest-performing structures based on algorithm behavior and engagement data. Each has a distinct use case.

---

## Format 1: Long-Form List

**Best for:** Sharing frameworks, lessons, tips, mistakes, or ranked insights
**Peak day:** Tuesday or Thursday (high-reach formats)
**Reaches:** Broadest audience — shareable, saveable, algorithmic reach

### Structure:
```
[Hook — list tease type, e.g. "7 things I wish I knew before..."]

[Optional: 1-sentence context]

1. [Insight — short headline]
[1-2 lines expanding the insight]

2. [Insight]
[1-2 lines]

[... up to 7-10 items]

[Payoff line — bring it together]

[CTA block]
```

### Rules:
- Each list item = one bold idea + 1-2 sentences of value
- No fluff items — every item must earn its spot
- If adding an image: square or vertical only, acts as a "YouTube thumbnail" — bold, high-contrast, contains the list title or core claim
- Final item should be the most surprising or contrarian one
- No more than 10 items — if you have more, you need two posts

## Format 2: Hook Stack

**Best for:** Punchy insight sequences, mindset shifts, or a series of related truths
**Peak day:** Monday or Friday
**Reaches:** Strong early engagement → algorithm push

### Structure:
```
[3-line hook]

[Short statement — 1 line]
[Short statement — 1 line]
[Short statement — 1 line]
[Short statement — 1 line]
[Short statement — 1 line]

[Payoff — the "so what" statement that ties the sequence together]

[CTA block]
```

### Rules:
- No numbers — this is rhythm-based, not list-based
- No transitions like "Additionally" or "Furthermore" — just statements
- Each line must stand alone as a shareable thought
- Short sentences. Max 10 words per line.
- The payoff is critical — without it, the stack feels unfinished
- Image is optional — the format works without it

## Format 3: Long-Form Image (Value Image Post)

**Best for:** Dense frameworks, system overviews, or any insight that's more powerful visualized
**Peak day:** Monday or Wednesday
**Reaches:** High save rate — people screenshot these

### Structure:
```
[Short 3-line hook]

[The image carries the content — everything visible without scrolling]

[1-2 lines below the image — the "caption" that adds context]

[CTA block]
```

### Image rules:
- Square (1080×1080) or vertical (1080×1350) — never landscape
- Dark background (#0B1220) + white text → high contrast
- Must be readable as a screenshot (no squinting required)
- Title: bold, large font (Poppins Bold 48px+)
- Content: maximum 40 words total on the image
- One visual metaphor or diagram — not a wall of bullet points
- Design in Figma using brand tokens — see `figma-frame-instructions.md`

## Format 4: Long-Form Carousel (PDF Document Post)

**Best for:** Step-by-step systems, frameworks with multiple parts, or educational content that needs visual structure
**Peak day:** Thursday (second-highest reach format)
**Reaches:** Highest organic reach format on LinkedIn in 2026

### Structure:
```
Slide 1 (Title): [Bold hook headline] + [Subheadline — "Here's the exact system:"]
Slides 2-8 (Value): One insight per slide — numbered, short headline + 3 bullets
Slide 9 (CTA): "Follow @[handle] for more [niche]" + Newsletter link
```

```
[Accompanying post text — separate from the carousel]

[3-line hook that makes the carousel feel unmissable]

[1-2 lines: "I built this into a 9-slide carousel. Everything you need is inside."]

[CTA: "♻️ Repost to share with your network."]
```

### Rules:
- Upload as a PDF to LinkedIn — this triggers the "document post" algorithm treatment
- Every slide must stand alone as a screenshot
- No slide over 40 words
- Final slide = follower CTA + newsletter CTA
- See `carousel-structure.md` in linkedin-carousel-creator for full slide blueprint

## Format 5: Personal Story (Behind-the-Scenes)

**Best for:** Building trust, humanizing, sharing failures/wins, and connecting at the identity level
**Peak day:** Wednesday
**Reaches:** High comment engagement — people respond to stories with their own

### Structure:
```
[Story hook — start in the middle of the moment, not at the beginning]
[e.g. "I almost deleted my LinkedIn account in March 2024."]

[The setup — 1-2 sentences: what was happening]

[The conflict or turning point — 1-3 sentences]

[The lesson — what you learned, distilled to 1-2 clear sentences]

[The application — how this applies to your reader]

[CTA block]
```

### Rules:
- Start in the moment — don't build up to it
- "I" / "I've" / "When" / "If" are your opening words — never "Are you..."
- Real details = credibility (name the month, the dollar amount, the client)
- Keep it 200-400 words — long enough to land, short enough to finish
- Never use: "journey", "leveled up", "passion", "hustle", "grind"
- One lesson per story — don't dilute it

## Format 6: Deep-Work System Reveal

**Best for:** Showcasing a methodology, process, or framework you use personally
**Peak day:** Tuesday or Thursday
**Reaches:** High save rate, strong authority signal

### Structure:
```
[Result-first or curiosity hook — "Here's the exact system I use to [result]"]

Here's how it works:

Step 1: [Name] — [1-2 sentence explanation]
Step 2: [Name] — [1-2 sentence explanation]
Step 3: [Name] — [1-2 sentence explanation]
[... 3-7 steps]

The full system takes [timeframe] to set up.
After that, it runs in [X] minutes per [day/week].

[Why this works — 1-2 sentences connecting to the reader's problem]

[CTA block]
```

### Rules:
- "Exact" and "system" in the hook — readers click for the specifics
- Steps must be specific enough to actually do — no vague verbs
- Include time investment (setup + maintenance) — people want to know if it's feasible
- Optional: include a screenshot or image of your actual system/tool setup
- This format builds the strongest authority over time — use at least 2x/month

---

## Format Selection Guide

| You want to... | Use this format |
|----------------|----------------|
| Reach the most new people | Long-Form List or Carousel |
| Build the most trust | Personal Story or Deep-Work System |
| Get the most saves | Long-Form Image or Carousel |
| Get the most comments | Personal Story or Contrarian Hook Stack |
| Convert to followers | System Reveal or Identity Hook Stack |
| Repurpose existing content | Hook Stack or List (extract key insights) |
