# DM Sequence Templates — 5 Outreach Types

> Every effective LinkedIn DM sequence has the same 3-message arc: open with specificity → deliver value with no ask → soft CTA. These templates apply that arc to 5 distinct outreach scenarios.

---

## The 3-Message Structure (All Types)

```
Message 1 — Open (T=0)
Goal: Get a reply. Nothing else.
Rule: Specific to them. Not generic. No pitch.
Length: 1-3 sentences max.

Message 2 — Value Drop (T+2-3 days after reply)
Goal: Give before you ask. Build trust.
Rule: Deliver something useful with zero ask attached.
Length: 2-4 sentences + resource/insight.

Message 3 — Soft CTA (T+3-5 days after Message 2)
Goal: Move to next step (call, content exchange, referral, etc.)
Rule: Single, clear, low-friction ask. Make it easy to say yes.
Length: 1-2 sentences + link or question.
```

---

## Sequence Type 1: Post Commenter (Warm)

**When to use:** Someone commented on your post (or you commented on theirs) and you want to continue the conversation.

**Context:** You have a warm signal — they engaged with your content. This is the easiest DM to send.

```
Message 1:
[Name], your comment on [brief reference to post topic] stuck with me.

[Specific reaction to their point in 1 sentence — not "great insight!", but a real response]

[Question that continues the conversation OR a related thought]

---

Message 2 (after reply):
Based on what you mentioned about [their point], I thought you'd find this useful:

[Resource: post link, tool, framework, or 1-paragraph insight directly relevant to their comment]

No agenda — just thought it was relevant to what you shared.

---

Message 3 (after Message 2 reply):
[Name], I'd love to learn more about [specific thing from their work/profile].

Would a 20-minute conversation make sense? [Calendar link]

If timing's off, no worries — happy to stay connected here.
```

---

## Sequence Type 2: New Follower (Warm)

**When to use:** Someone follows you. You notice something specific about their profile worth engaging.

**Context:** They signaled interest in you. This is a warm lead you can activate.

```
Message 1:
[Name], thanks for the follow.

I noticed you're working on [specific thing from their profile or recent post] — that's a space I think about a lot.

[One question or observation about their work]

---

Message 2 (after reply):
[Name], appreciate you sharing that.

Given what you mentioned, I thought this might be useful:
[Resource — your post, framework, tool, or short insight]

This is something I've seen work well for [identity similar to them].

---

Message 3 (after Message 2 reply):
If you're ever open to it, I'd love to trade notes on [shared topic].

20 minutes — no pitch. [Calendar link]

Or we can just keep the conversation here. Either works.
```

---

## Sequence Type 3: Cold Outreach (Cold)

**When to use:** You want to connect with someone who doesn't know you. They match your ICP or are someone you genuinely want to build a relationship with.

**Context:** No prior engagement. Must earn the right to their attention with the very first sentence.

```
Message 1:
[Name], [specific observation about their work — not their job title, but something they actually did or said]

[One-sentence value or insight relevant to their current situation]

[Single low-friction ask — not a pitch, not "can I pick your brain", but a yes/no question]

---

Message 2 (after reply):
[Name], thanks for getting back to me.

[Deliver the thing you teased or a relevant resource based on their reply]

No agenda with this — just saw a clear connection to what you mentioned.

---

Message 3 (after Message 2 reply):
[Name], I've been thinking about [their specific situation].

I'd love to [specific ask: swap notes / get your perspective / explore whether there's a fit].

20 minutes worth it for you? [Link] — or just let me know a time.
```

**Cold DM Rules:**
- Never mention your product or service in Message 1
- Never say "I'd love to hop on a quick call" in Message 1
- The first message has one job: get a reply
- Specific > complimentary. "I read your post on X" > "I love your content"

## Sequence Type 4: Post-Meeting Follow-Up

**When to use:** After a call, event, or conversation — you want to deepen the relationship or move toward a next step.

**Context:** You have shared context. Use it.

```
Message 1 (within 24 hours of meeting):
[Name], great conversation today.

The thing you said about [specific point from conversation] — I've been thinking about it since we hung up.

[Related thought or question that shows you were genuinely listening]

---

Message 2 (3-5 days later):
[Name], based on what you mentioned about [their situation], I pulled this together:

[Resource, framework, or short write-up directly related to what they discussed]

Thought this might be useful given where you are with [their goal/challenge].

---

Message 3 (1 week later):
[Name], [next step you discussed on the call] — I'd love to actually move on this.

[Specific proposal or calendar link]

[Fallback: "If timing shifted, no worries — just let me know."]
```

---

## Sequence Type 5: Referral Ask

**When to use:** You want to ask a connection to refer you to someone in their network or to vouch for your work.

**Context:** This is the most delicate type — you're asking for social capital. Make it specific and easy to say yes.

```
Message 1:
[Name], I wanted to reach out directly because I trust your judgment on this.

I'm currently [what you're working on / who you're looking to meet].

Is there anyone in your network who [specific description — not "any founders" but "B2B SaaS founders at Series A who are building their personal brand"]?

---

Message 2 (after reply / if they said "let me think"):
[Name], no rush on this — I appreciate you even considering it.

To make it easy: here's a one-paragraph intro you could forward if it ever feels right:

"[Name], I want to connect you with [your name]. [1-sentence what you do]. [1-sentence social proof]. I think you two should talk. [Your LinkedIn URL]"

Feel free to use, modify, or ignore entirely.

---

Message 3 (if no intro after 1 week):
[Name], I know this one might be a stretch — no pressure at all.

If it ever comes to mind, I'd be grateful. In the meantime, I'd love to return the favor: is there anyone in my network who could be useful to you right now?
```

---

## Universal DM Rules

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Reference something specific about them | Use "I hope this message finds you well" |
| Deliver value before making an ask | Pitch in Message 1 |
| Ask one question at a time | Ask multiple questions in one message |
| Keep Message 1 under 3 sentences | Write paragraphs |
| Follow up once if no reply (Message 1 only) | Follow up more than twice |
| Make the CTA low-friction (yes/no question or 1-click calendar) | Use vague CTAs ("let me know if interested") |
