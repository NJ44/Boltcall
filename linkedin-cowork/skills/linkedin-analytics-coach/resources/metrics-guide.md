# LinkedIn Metrics Guide — Benchmarks, Interpretation & What to Track

> Not all metrics are created equal. This guide tells you which numbers actually matter, what they mean, and what to do when they're off.

---

## The Metrics That Matter (Ranked by Importance)

| Rank | Metric | Why It Matters |
|------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | Repost rate | #1 algorithm signal — drives non-follower reach |
| 2 | Early engagement (first 45 min) | Triggers algorithmic push or caps distribution |
| 3 | Profile-to-follower conversion | Determines if your profile converts visitors |
| 4 | Comment rate | Quality engagement signal — long comments > likes |
| 5 | Monthly impressions per follower | Measures algorithm reach beyond your existing audience |
| 6 | Follower growth per month | Compound growth metric — slow growth = leaky funnel |
| 7 | Profile views per week | Indicates content-to-profile pipeline health |

## Metric Deep Dives

### 1. Repost Rate

**Formula:** Reposts ÷ Impressions × 100

**Why it's #1:** A repost distributes your post to a completely new network. LinkedIn treats reposts as the strongest signal of content quality.

**Benchmark:** 2%+ is great. Below 0.5% = your content isn't shareable enough.

**What causes low repost rate:**
- No viral trigger (nothing that makes someone want their network to see it)
- Too personal / too niche (valuable to you, not to your reader's peers)
- No repost prompt in CTA ("♻️ Repost if..." tells people what to do)
- Content that's good but not *share-worthy* (readable ≠ repostable)

**Fix:** Add a Data Surprise or Identity Reinforcement trigger + explicit repost CTA on next post.

---

### 2. Early Engagement (First 45 Minutes)

**What to measure:** Total reactions + comments in the 45 minutes after publishing

**Why it matters:** LinkedIn's algorithm uses this as the primary quality signal. High velocity = algorithm pushes to non-followers. Low velocity = capped at current followers.

**Benchmark:** 30+ is great. Below 10 = your audience isn't engaging or you posted at the wrong time.

**What causes low early engagement:**
- Wrong posting time (not 8am or 12pm Tue-Thu)
- Content doesn't resonate with current followers (wrong category or hook)
- No engagement group to seed initial velocity
- Posted via scheduler (algorithm deprioritizes API posts)

**Fix:** Post at peak time (8am Tue/Thu), notify your 3-5 most engaged followers, reply to every comment within 45 minutes.

---

### 3. Profile-to-Follower Conversion

**Formula:** New followers in a period ÷ Profile views in same period × 100

**Why it matters:** Every post sends traffic to your profile. If the profile doesn't convert, you're leaving followers (and future customers) on the table.

**Benchmark:** 19%+ is great. Below 5% = profile is not working.

**What causes low conversion:**
- Creator Mode OFF (Connect button instead of Follow button)
- Weak headline (no one-liner word ownership)
- Featured section missing or pointing to wrong things
- About section opens with "I am a [title]"

**Fix:** Enable Creator Mode first (highest leverage), then fix the Featured section.

---

### 4. Comment Rate

**Formula:** Comments ÷ Impressions × 100

**Why it matters:** Comments are worth ~5x more than likes in LinkedIn's algorithm. A post that generates real conversation gets pushed further than a post with only reactions.

**What causes low comment rate:**
- Post doesn't end with a question or conversational prompt
- Topic is too educational (people learn, don't respond)
- Hook doesn't create curiosity or controversy

**Fix:** Add a question in the body (not the CTA) that your ICP can answer in 1-2 sentences. Make it specific: "What's the one thing on this list you're doing differently?" beats "What do you think?"

---

### 5. Monthly Impressions Per Follower

**Formula:** Total monthly impressions ÷ Follower count

**Why it matters:** Measures how far beyond your existing audience your content reaches.

**Benchmark:** 10x is average. Below 5x = your content is only reaching existing followers (no algorithm amplification).

**What causes low ratio:**
- No reposts (content stays within existing network)
- No viral triggers (algorithm doesn't distribute to topic feeds)
- Inconsistent posting (algorithm reduces baseline distribution for dormant accounts)

**Fix:** Focus on repost rate (it's the primary driver of impressions beyond your follower base).

---

### 6. Follower Growth Per Month

**What it measures:** Raw follower acquisition — the compound growth metric.

**Benchmark:** At scale, 11,000+/month is great. Early stage (under 1K followers): 100-500/month is realistic.

**Important:** Follower growth is a lagging indicator — it responds to changes in post quality and profile conversion 2-4 weeks later.

**What causes stagnant follower growth:**
- Profile not converting (Creator Mode off, no Featured section)
- Content not reaching non-followers (low repost rate, low impressions/follower ratio)
- Inconsistent posting (algorithm deprioritizes dormant accounts)

---

### 7. Profile Views Per Week

**What it measures:** How many people clicked through to your profile from posts, search, or recommendations.

**Why it matters:** Profile views are the top of the follower conversion funnel. If views are high but new followers are low → profile conversion problem. If views are low → reach problem.

**Benchmark:** 2,000+/week is great.

## How to Read a Single Post's Performance

| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| High impressions, low engagement | Hook worked, body didn't land | Rewrite body — clearer value per line |
| Low impressions, high engagement rate | Saw it, liked it — but few people saw it | Wrong posting time or format for peak day |
| High comments, low reposts | Conversation starter but not shareable | Add data or identity trigger to next version |
| High reposts, low comments | Shareable concept, not provocative | Add a discussion question |
| Strong first 45 min, then nothing | Algorithm didn't push — engagement group needed | Build a 5-person engagement group |
| Viral spike then drop to baseline | One-hit content, no system | Build a 2-week calendar to maintain consistency |

---

## 3-Number Weekly Review

Every week, track just these 3:

1. **Best post repost rate** — did any post hit 2%+?
2. **Early engagement on your peak-day post** — did you hit 30+ in 45 min?
3. **New followers this week** — trending up, flat, or down?

If all 3 are good: repeat the format + hook type that performed.
If one is off: identify the root cause from the benchmarks above, make one change, test next week.
