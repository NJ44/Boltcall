# Carousel Structure — Slide-by-Slide Blueprint

> LinkedIn carousels (document posts) are the highest organic reach format in 2026. They're uploaded as PDFs, which triggers LinkedIn's "document post" algorithm treatment — giving them significantly more distribution than standard image posts.

---

## Why Carousels Work

- LinkedIn distributes document posts more broadly than image posts
- Each slide swipe is counted as an engagement signal
- High-value carousels get saved and revisited
- Every slide that contains the creator's brand reinforces identity

---

## Carousel Anatomy

A standard 9-slide carousel structure:

| Slide | Role | Content |
|-------|------|---------|
| Slide 1 | Title / Hook | Bold headline + subheadline |
| Slides 2-8 | Value | One insight per slide |
| Slide 9 | CTA | Follow + Newsletter |

---

## Slide Specifications

**Canvas size:** 1080×1080px (square) — square only, never landscape
**Background:** Dark (#0B1220)
**Primary text:** White (#FFFFFF)
**Accent color:** Brand blue (#2563EB)
**Sky accent (slide numbers, subtle elements):** #93C5FD
**Font family:** Poppins
**Export:** PNG at 2x resolution, then compiled to PDF

---

## Slide 1 — Title Slide

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll. Make the topic instantly legible.

```
Layout:
- Top: Slide number label [optional — some skip on title slide]
- Center: HEADLINE in Poppins Bold, 48-56px, white, 1-2 lines max
- Below: Subheadline in Poppins Regular 24px, muted white or sky (#93C5FD)
- Bottom: Thin accent line (#2563EB) spanning width
- Optional: Brand mark or handle (@) in small text, bottom right

Content:
- Headline = the hook (same as post hook or a shorter version)
- Subheadline = "A [N]-slide breakdown" or "Here's the exact [topic]"
- Max 15 words total on this slide
```

**Example:**
```
HEADLINE: 9 LinkedIn Rules Nobody Tells You
SUBHEADLINE: (I learned these the hard way)
```

---

## Slides 2-8 — Value Slides

**Purpose:** Deliver one insight per slide. Readers should be able to screenshot any slide and share it independently.

```
Layout:
- Top left: Slide number in sky blue (#93C5FD), Poppins Bold 20px
- Below number: INSIGHT TITLE in white, Poppins Bold 36px
- Below title: 2-3 bullet points OR 1-2 sentences of explanation
- Optional: Icon or simple visual element right side

Content rules:
- ONE insight per slide — never two
- Headline: 4-8 words, bold claim, not a question
- Body: max 40 words total
- Each slide must stand alone — assume reader screenshotted it
- Progressive structure: each slide should build on the previous

Bullet style:
- → Arrow or — dash (not dots)
- 1 line per bullet, max 10 words each
- Maximum 3 bullets per slide
```

**Value Slide Example:**
```
02
The Algorithm Doesn't Care About Likes
→ Reposts are the #1 signal
→ One repost = your content in a new network
→ Engineer reposts, not reactions
```

---

## Slide 9 — CTA Slide

**Purpose:** Convert slide readers into followers and newsletter subscribers.

```
Layout:
- Background: Brand blue (#2563EB) — full bleed, color flip for visual break
- Center: CTA headline in white, Poppins Bold 40px
- Below: Handle in large type
- Below: Newsletter URL or lead magnet URL
- Optional: Headshot in circle, bottom right

Content:
- Line 1: "Follow @[handle]"
- Line 2: "for [niche one-liner]"
- Line 3: Newsletter CTA — "[Newsletter Name] → [URL]"

Maximum 25 words
```

**CTA Slide Example:**
```
Follow @boltcall
for LinkedIn systems that drive revenue.

The Operator newsletter → boltcall.com/newsletter
```

---

## Accompanying Post Text

Every carousel needs a separate post text that goes above it. This is NOT the carousel — it's the caption.

```
Structure:
[3-line hook — use the same hook from the Title slide, but rewrite it]
[1-2 lines: tease what's inside]
[CTA: "♻️ Repost to share with your [identity] network."]

Example:
9 LinkedIn rules took me 3 years to learn.
None of them were in any course.
I put all of them into this 9-slide carousel.
♻️ Repost for any founder building their brand.
```

## Carousel Lengths

| Length | When to use |
|--------|------------|
| 5 slides | Tight frameworks, 3-4 rules, "quick breakdown" |
| 7 slides | Standard — 5 value slides + title + CTA |
| 9 slides | Full system reveal — 7 value slides + title + CTA |
| 12+ slides | Deep-dive content — reserved for pillar posts |

**Minimum:** 5 slides (1 title + 3 value + 1 CTA)
**Maximum:** 15 slides (LinkedIn document limit)

---

## Quality Checklist Before Publishing

- [ ] Slide 1 headline reads as a standalone hook
- [ ] Every value slide (2-8) can be screenshotted and understood without context
- [ ] No slide exceeds 40 words
- [ ] Slide 9 has a clear follow + newsletter CTA
- [ ] Accent color (#2563EB) appears on every slide for brand consistency
- [ ] Exported as PDF (not image sequence) for document post algorithm treatment
- [ ] Accompanying post text is written separately
