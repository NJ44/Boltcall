# Profile Audit Checklist — 20-Point Rubric

> Score each element: ✅ Done well | ⚠️ Needs improvement | ❌ Missing or wrong
> Target: 18-20 ✅ for 19%+ profile-to-follower conversion rate

---

## Layer 1: Visual Layer (First Impression Before Reading)

### 1. Headshot
- [ ] Professional quality — not a logo, screenshot, or casual photo
- [ ] Portrait orientation — face clearly visible and centered
- [ ] Good lighting — window light or professional lighting preferred
- [ ] Consistent with your brand's authority level

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Note:** [What's working or what needs to change]

---

### 2. Banner (Cover Photo)
- [ ] Custom design — not the default LinkedIn blue/grey gradient
- [ ] Contains a clear CTA visible without scrolling (newsletter signup, lead magnet, one-liner)
- [ ] Brand colors and typography consistent with other content
- [ ] Text is readable at banner thumbnail size

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Note:** If no banner CTA → estimated 30-40% loss in lead magnet conversions from profile

---

### 3. Creator Mode: Enabled
- [ ] Creator Mode is ON
- [ ] Follow button visible (not Connect)
- [ ] Creator topics set (3 relevant hashtags/topics)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Note:** Creator Mode OFF = Connect button only = ~5% conversion. Creator Mode ON = Follow button = ~19% conversion. This is the single highest-leverage profile change.

---

## Layer 2: Identity Layer (Headline + One-Liner)

### 4. Headline — Owns One Word
- [ ] Contains a single ownable word or concept (Systems / Revenue / Growth / AI / Deep Work)
- [ ] Not just a job title ("Founder at X" → low conversion)
- [ ] Immediately legible what you do and for whom

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Formula:** [Ownable word] | [Value proposition for ICP]
**Example:** Systems | Helping B2B founders scale with organic content

---

### 5. Headline — Value Proposition Clarity
- [ ] Clearly states who benefits from following/connecting (ICP)
- [ ] Implies a specific outcome (not vague "helps businesses grow")
- [ ] ≤220 characters

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 6. Headline — Profile Search Optimization
- [ ] Contains the primary keyword your ICP would search for
- [ ] No filler words ("passionate about", "experienced in")
- [ ] Reads as a position statement, not a resume line

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

## Layer 3: Conversion Layer (Featured Section)

### 7. Featured Section — Present
- [ ] Featured section is visible on profile
- [ ] At least 1 item featured

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Note:** Featured section is the #2 conversion driver after Creator Mode

---

### 8. Featured Section — Newsletter Link (Item 1)
- [ ] Newsletter is the first featured item
- [ ] Thumbnail is a value image (not headshot) — high contrast, contains the newsletter name and value proposition
- [ ] URL goes directly to newsletter subscription page (not homepage)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 9. Featured Section — Lead Magnet or Product (Item 2)
- [ ] Second featured item links to primary lead magnet or core offer
- [ ] Thumbnail clearly communicates what's being offered
- [ ] Maximum 2 featured items (more = attention dilution)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

## Layer 4: About Section

### 10. About — Opening Line (Hook)
- [ ] First sentence is a HOOK — not "I am a [title]..."
- [ ] Creates curiosity, shares a surprising result, or asks a provocative question
- [ ] "See more" click-worthy without knowing anything else about you

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Good example:** "I built a $200K consulting business from 50K LinkedIn followers. Here's what I've learned:"
**Bad example:** "I am a marketing consultant with 10 years of experience..."

---

### 11. About — Credibility Block
- [ ] Contains specific numbers (followers, revenue, clients helped, years of experience)
- [ ] Mentions 1-2 recognizable brands or outcomes
- [ ] Earns trust in the first read-through

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 12. About — Hook Stacking (3 Punchy Lines)
- [ ] Contains 3 short, punchy statements about your philosophy, method, or worldview
- [ ] Each line stands alone as a shareable thought
- [ ] Feels like the creator, not a resume

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 13. About — 3 CTAs
- [ ] Newsletter CTA with URL
- [ ] Lead magnet or product CTA with URL
- [ ] Social or contact CTA (DM me / book a call / email)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

## Layer 5: Trust Signals

### 14. Custom LinkedIn URL
- [ ] URL is customized (not linkedin.com/in/abc123def456)
- [ ] Matches your brand handle or full name

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 15. Pronouns Set
- [ ] Pronouns field is filled in
- [ ] Small trust signal — shows profile completeness and human authenticity

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 16. Recommendations Received
- [ ] 3+ recommendations from recognizable people (clients, colleagues, partners)
- [ ] Recommendations mention specific results — not just generic "great to work with"

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 17. Skills Section
- [ ] Top 5 skills are relevant to your ICP's search terms (not soft skills)
- [ ] Skills have endorsements from relevant connections

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

## Layer 6: Activity & Distribution

### 18. Posting Publicly
- [ ] Recent posts visible on profile (not "connections only")
- [ ] Post frequency shows consistency (not sporadic posting)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

### 19. LinkedIn Newsletter Active
- [ ] Newsletter is launched and visible on profile
- [ ] At least 1 issue published

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
**Note:** LinkedIn Newsletter gets algorithmic distribution to all followers + appears in LinkedIn search and Google search

---

### 20. All Sections Complete
- [ ] No blank sections visible when viewing profile
- [ ] Experience section has at least current role with description
- [ ] Education filled in
- [ ] Company page linked (if applicable)

**Score:** ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌

---

## Conversion Rate Estimate

| ✅ Count | Conversion Rate | Rating |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| 0-5 | < 5% | Poor |
| 6-10 | 5-10% | Below average |
| 11-14 | 10-15% | Average |
| 15-17 | 15-19% | Good |
| 18-20 | 19%+ | Great |

---

## Priority Fix Order

When fixes are needed, address in this order (highest conversion impact first):

1. **Creator Mode** — enables Follow button (5% → 19% conversion)
2. **Featured Section** — drives newsletter + lead magnet
3. **Banner with CTA** — visible before any scrolling
4. **About Section opening line** — hook or forfeit
5. **Headline formula** — one word ownership + ICP value prop
