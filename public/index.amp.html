Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does Boltcall offer?

Boltcall gives local businesses a complete AI system that handles all the stuff that keeps slipping through the cracks. It replies to new leads in seconds, answers calls and messages, books appointments directly on your calendar, and follows up automatically so no one gets forgotten.

Can it transfer the call to a real person?

Yes. Set live-transfer rules (e.g., during business hours, hot leads only). If no one answers, it books or takes a voicemail and alerts you.

How good is the call quality and voice?

Natural and clear. You pick a voice and tone (friendly, calm, formal). We keep replies short so calls feel human, not robotic.

Can it use my current phone number?

Yes. You can forward your existing number to your AI receptionist, or buy a new number inside the app and start fresh.
